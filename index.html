The Olympic Games have started in Tokyo and we’ve been asking some of the players at our training camp in Surrey which of the events they’d most like to win a gold medal in. Young forward Anthony Elanga admitted that the blue-riband 100m race was the one he’d have his eye on, along with the longer 1,500m, although team-mate Jesse Lingard thought he’d be better suited to the long jump.
